About Us

Make Things Not Ads is a social media marketing consultancy based in Richmond, Virginia. Established in 2025, we're at the forefront of using AI to help businesses create efficient, authentic content that resonates with audiences.

We believe in creating genuine connections through social media—not just advertisements. Our approach combines creative strategy with cutting-edge technology to deliver results that matter.

Our AI Ethics

Transparency

We believe in full transparency about how AI is used in our work. We always disclose when content is AI-assisted and ensure clients understand the technology.

Human-Centered

AI is a tool to enhance human creativity, not replace it. We maintain the perfect balance between AI efficiency and human authenticity in all our work.

Privacy First

We never use AI tools that compromise user data or privacy. Our AI processes are designed with privacy and security as foundational principles.

At Make Things Not Ads, we're committed to responsible AI use. We continuously evaluate our AI tools and processes to ensure they align with our ethical standards and deliver authentic value to our clients and their audiences.

We believe that the future of marketing lies in the ethical application of artificial intelligence, where technology enhances human connection rather than diminishes it.
